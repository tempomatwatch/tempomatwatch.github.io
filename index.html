Not meant to be just another watch.

The design is an exercise in reduction, stripping away every non-essential element. The dial is bare bronze—a material traditional to watchmaking, yet presented here without the usual ornaments, numbers, and logos. It’s a deliberate choice, a reminder that time itself is the treasure, not the ballast we adorn it with.

It is a symbol for the most valuable thing I own.

All I have to decide is what to do with the time that is given to me.

As an automatic watch, it is powered by the movement of its wearer. This is not a metaphor; the mechanism runs on your time. Once you stop moving, the watch stops too, waiting for you to continue your journey.

  • Automatic mechanism
  • 42-hour power reserve
  • 200m water resistance
  • 316L Solid stainless steel

This is a finely crafted timepiece, made to accompany you everywhere you spend your time. It is powered by a robust Japanese automatic mechanism and is hand assembled in Slovenia.

Time is the most valuable thing I can spend

Time is priceless. This watch is not. It is a symbol for time and not a symbol for luxury or status.
